About Wanfex Exports

Excellence in Agricultural Exports Since Inception

Founded with a vision to showcase India's agricultural excellence, Wanfex Exports is a growing name in the fresh fruits and spices export industry. Our focus is on quality, transparency, and reliability.

We specialize in delivering farm-fresh Nagpur Oranges, Premium Bananas, high-quality Turmeric, and premium Turmeric Powder to global markets. Sourced directly from India's fertile farms, our products are 100% natural, hygienically processed, and packed to international export standards.
